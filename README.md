# Working Knowledge

A Project Zomboid Build 42 mod. Knox County's filing cabinets are full of the working knowledge of people who aren't around to use it anymore — safety manuals, training guides, certification handbooks. Find one, read it, and carry a little of that forward.

## How It Works

Documents found in filing cabinets can be read once per character. Reading one grants a flat XP boost toward the associated skill — no level requirement, no page-grinding. The boost is modest (roughly 25% of what it takes to hit level 1), meant to supplement your experience rather than replace it. The document stays in your inventory afterward and can be given to another survivor.

Skill books and all other XP sources stack normally.

## Finding Documents

Look in filing cabinets. Different documents appear in contextually appropriate rooms — a fleet maintenance log turns up in a car dealership, not a hospital. Spawn rates are intentionally low; finding one should feel like a find.
