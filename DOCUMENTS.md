# Working Knowledge — Document Catalogue

Each entry lists the item ID, display name, associated skill (Perks enum), intended spawn containers, and flavor text.

Spawn weight across all entries is 2 (rare find). Adjust per item once playtesting informs balance.

## Crafting and Trade

**WK_LumberYardManual** — Lumber Yard Safety Manual — `Woodwork`
Spawn: filing cabinets in warehouses, lumber yards, construction trailers
> A laminated pocket guide issued to new hires at a lumber yard. Covers hand and power tool safety, load-bearing limits, and material grading. Someone highlighted the section on avoiding kickback.

**WK_ElectricalCodeRef** — Residential Electrical Code Reference — `Electricity`
Spawn: filing cabinets in offices, hardware stores, contractor supply shops
> A worn spiral-bound summary of the NEC, annotated in red pen throughout. Whoever used this knew what they were doing — the useful pages are dog-eared, the rest ignored.

**WK_VehicleMaintenanceGuide** — Fleet Vehicle Maintenance Guide — `Mechanics`
Spawn: filing cabinets in car dealerships, auto shops, transport depots, gas stations
> A binder of standardized maintenance checklists and service intervals from a regional transport company. Includes fluid specs, brake inspection procedures, and a troubleshooting index.

**WK_AlterationsGuide** — Alterations & Repair: A Professional Reference — `Tailoring`
Spawn: filing cabinets in clothing stores, dry cleaners, tailor shops
> A trade reference for garment alteration work — seam allowances, fabric behavior, stitching techniques for different materials. Tucked inside is a handwritten note about a customer's wedding dress that was never picked up.

**WK_FoodHandlerGuide** — Food Handler Certification Study Guide — `Cooking`
Spawn: filing cabinets in restaurants, school cafeterias, grocery stores, bars
> The study booklet for Knox County's food handler certification exam. Heavy on food safety and temperature control, but the prep techniques section is genuinely useful.

**WK_WeldingHandbook** — Welding Safety & Technique Handbook — `MetalWelding`
Spawn: filing cabinets in factories, auto body shops, metal fabrication shops
> Issued by a welding supply distributor. Covers arc, MIG, and TIG basics, with a strong emphasis on PPE and ventilation. The burn marks on the cover suggest it lived near an actual welding station.

**WK_MasonryReference** — Concrete & Masonry Field Reference — `Masonry`
Spawn: filing cabinets in construction company offices, hardware stores
> A pocket-sized field reference for mixing ratios, curing times, and block coursing. Spiral-bound so it lays flat. Covered in concrete dust.

**WK_BlacksmithPrimer** — Introduction to Metalworking — `Blacksmith`
Spawn: store counters in hardware stores, machine shops, vocational schools
> A beginner's guide from a community college continuing education program. Covers tool identification, metal properties, and basic shaping techniques. The binding is cracked from use.

**WK_MaintenanceSOP** — Facilities Maintenance Standard Operating Procedures — `Maintenance`
Spawn: filing cabinets in offices, schools, hospitals, factories
> An internal SOP binder from a facilities management company. Covers HVAC filters, plumbing basics, door hardware, and a lot of liability disclaimers. More useful than it looks.

**WK_HandloadersManual** — Handloader's Reference Manual — `Reloading`

**WK_SpearfishingGuide** — Spearfishing: Techniques and Safety — `Spear`

**WK_HuntersFieldReference** — Hunter's Field Reference — `Tracking`

> A compact field guide to reading sign, identifying tracks, and understanding game movement. Written for deer hunters but the underlying skills are broader.
